Performance UGC · Pay only when content performs

Context

Why brands are tired of the usual playbook

Paid ads

  • Often $8–15+ CPM before creative and management
  • Ad account risk, bans, and auction volatility
  • You rent reach — when the spend stops, the asset is gone

Traditional influencers

  • Flat fees up front — results are a hope, not a guarantee
  • One or few posts, not a flood of organic angles
  • Hard to scale without renegotiating every time
The offer

Pay per 1,000 organic views — not flat creator fees

  • $1,000 budget → 1M+ views guaranteed (minimum before your campaign runs — same framing as the main site)
  • Creators post from their own accounts on short-form platforms — no "Sponsored" positioning
  • Hard budget cap — spend does not exceed what you set
How it works

Four steps from budget to views

01
Fund a campaign budget
You set the cap. Guaranteed minimum views are agreed before launch.
02
Creators post
Many creators ship original clips from their accounts across platforms.
03
Manual review
Every clip is reviewed. Off-brand or low quality does not count toward spend.
04
Pay for views that count
Only approved views deduct from your budget. Track clips in real time.

Brief to the network in 24–48 hours. First clips often within ~16 hours. Many campaigns are live in 48–72 hours after onboarding.
