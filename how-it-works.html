How It Works

Simple process.
Zero guesswork.

Set a budget. We brief 70,000+ creators. They post. You only pay when content hits your view threshold.

01
Step One
You fund a campaign budget
Set your budget — that's your hard cap. At $1 RPM, every $1,000 guarantees a minimum of 1,000,000 views. You will not spend a cent over your cap without approval.

02
Step Two
Creators post content about your brand
Your campaign launches inside our community of 70,000+ creators. They produce original short-form videos on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube — from their own accounts, organically. No ad accounts. No "Sponsored" labels. No product shipping needed.
03
Step Three
We manually review every submission
Before any clip counts toward your budget, our team reviews it. Wrong tone, wrong audience, off-brand, low quality — rejected instantly. You never pay for content that doesn't meet your standard.
04
Step Four
Pay only for what performs
Approved clips pay out at $1 RPM — only after hitting the minimum view threshold. Clips that don't qualify still stay live and keep pulling organic views. You don't pay for them. A per-clip cap means one viral video can never drain your budget.

05
Step Five
Content keeps compounding after budget ends
Every clip stays live permanently. Long after your budget is spent, those posts keep pulling organic views and driving traffic. You also own every clip — repurpose the best ones as paid ad creatives or repost them to your own channels.
The RPM Model Explained

Three mechanics that
protect your budget.

$1 RPM
Creators earn $1 per 1,000 views. A $1,000 budget guarantees a minimum of 1,000,000 paid views. Simple, predictable, easy to model.
Minimum View Threshold
Every clip must hit a minimum view count before it qualifies for payout. Clips that don't qualify stay live — generating free views on top of your paid minimum.
Per-Clip Payout Cap
Every clip has a maximum payout. Even if a video goes viral with 10M views, it can only earn up to the cap. Your budget is spread across hundreds of creators — not drained by one.
