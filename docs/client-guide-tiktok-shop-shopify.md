# Connect your Shopify store to TikTok Shop

When you finish this guide, your **TikTok sales channel** will be installed in Shopify and linked to **TikTok Shop**, so you can sync your catalog and manage TikTok Shop orders from Shopify alongside your other channels.

This guide covers TikTok’s **official** Shopify app: the [TikTok sales channel on the Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/tiktok).

---

## Before you start

Check these items **before** you install the channel. If something is missing, fix it first to avoid errors during setup.

- **Business address:** You have a **verifiable address** for your store in Shopify under **Settings → [Locations](https://admin.shopify.com/settings/locations)**.
- **Supported country:** Your store is in one of these countries: **United States, United Kingdom, Spain, Ireland, France, Italy, Germany, Mexico, Japan, or Brazil**.
- **Online store:** You use Shopify’s **online store**.
- **TikTok for Business:** You have or can create a **TikTok for Business** account during setup (see Step 6).
- **Return policy:** Your store **shows a return policy** to customers. If you need help, see Shopify’s guide on [return rules and return policy](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/fulfillment/managing-orders/returns/return-rules).
- **Products:** Your products follow **TikTok Shop rules** for your region. Use the policy for your market:
  - [United States — Prohibited products](https://seller-us.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=1399532709988097&identity=1)
  - [United Kingdom — Prohibited products](https://seller-uk.tiktok.com/university/essay?identity=1&role=1&knowledge_id=7753844603979521&from=policy)
  - [Spain — EU prohibited products](https://seller-es.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6701244974499617&identity=1)
  - [Ireland — EU prohibited products](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6671917692815137&identity=1)
  - [France — EU prohibited products](https://seller-fr.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6711589562615585&identity=1)
  - [Italy — EU prohibited products](https://seller-it.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6711589562894113&identity=1)
  - [Germany — EU prohibited products](https://seller-de.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6636273306945313&identity=1)
  - [Mexico — Prohibited products](https://seller-mx.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8357698199127824)
  - [Japan — Prohibited products](https://seller-jp.tiktok.com/university/essay?identity=1&role=1&knowledge_id=2721296229418769&from=policy)
  - [Brazil — Prohibited products](https://seller-br.tiktok.com/university/essay?identity=1&role=1&knowledge_id=6483182812481296&from=policy)

## Step-by-step: Install and connect

Complete these steps **in order**. Use a **desktop browser** and stay logged into the **Shopify store owner** or **staff account** that is allowed to install sales channels.

### 1. Open Sales channels in Shopify

1. Log in to your **[Shopify admin](https://admin.shopify.com/)**.
2. Click **Settings** (gear icon, usually bottom left).
3. Click **Sales channels**.
4. Click **Shopify App Store**.  
   - If Shopify asks you to log in again, sign in to continue.

<!-- IMAGE: 02-settings-sales-channels.png — Settings → Sales channels, with Shopify App Store visible. -->

### 2. Find and add the TikTok channel

1. In the **Shopify App Store**, use the search box and search for **TikTok**.
2. Open the official **TikTok** sales channel listing (from TikTok).
3. Click **Add channel** (wording may appear as **Add app** or **Add sales channel** depending on Shopify’s layout — choose the option that installs the TikTok channel).

<!-- IMAGE: 03-app-store-search-tiktok.png — App Store search results for “TikTok”. -->

<!-- IMAGE: 04-tiktok-app-add-channel.png — TikTok app page showing Add channel / Add app. -->

### 3. Start TikTok Shop setup

1. You should land on a **Set up TikTok Shop** (or similar) page inside Shopify.
2. In the **TikTok Shop** section, click **Connect**.

<!-- IMAGE: 05-setup-tiktok-shop-connect.png — Set up TikTok Shop page with Connect under TikTok Shop. -->

### 4. Sign in or register with TikTok

A **Sign Up** dialog will open.

- If you **already have** a TikTok for Business account: click **Log in** and complete sign-in.
- If you **do not** have an account yet: enter **Phone number**, **Email**, and **Password**, then click **Register as merchant** and follow any prompts TikTok shows.

<!-- IMAGE: 06-tiktok-sign-up-dialog.png — Sign Up dialog with Log in vs Register as merchant. -->

### 5. Allow Shopify to connect

1. When you see **Connect to Shopify?** (or similar), read the **TikTok Shop Merchant Terms of Service**.
2. Click **Connect** to approve the link between TikTok Shop and your Shopify store.

<!-- IMAGE: 07-connect-to-shopify-dialog.png — Connect to Shopify consent dialog with Connect button. -->

### 6. Verify your shop

1. In the **Shop information** section, click **Verify**.
2. In the **Upload Documents** dialog, fill in every **required** field.
3. Click **Next** and complete any remaining screens TikTok requests.

<!-- IMAGE: 08-shop-information-verify.png — Shop information with Verify. -->

<!-- IMAGE: 09-upload-documents.png — Upload Documents dialog (blur IDs and sensitive data). -->

### 7. Finish in Shopify

1. Click **Finish setup** (or the final confirmation Shopify/TikTok shows) to complete the wizard.
2. You should now see **TikTok** listed under your sales channels in Shopify. Open it anytime from **Settings → Sales channels** or from the **Apps and sales channels** area in the admin sidebar.

<!-- IMAGE: 10-tiktok-in-sidebar.png — TikTok channel listed after installation (sidebar or sales channels list). -->

## If your admin looks different

Some Shopify stores show **Apps and sales channels** in the **left sidebar** first. If you do not see **Settings → Sales channels** easily:

1. Click **Apps and sales channels** in the sidebar.
2. Open the **Shopify App Store** from there.
3. Search for **TikTok** and add the same official channel.

The app is the same; only the **entry point** in the admin changes.

---

## After you connect

1. **TikTok Shop Seller Center:** Log in to [TikTok Shop Seller Center](https://seller-us.tiktok.com/) (use the seller URL for your region if different) and finish any steps TikTok still requires (business profile, verification, selling regions).
2. **Sync products:** In Shopify’s TikTok channel settings, choose how to sync your catalog — for example **all products** or **specific collections**. Updates from Shopify (price, inventory, images, descriptions) can stay in sync with TikTok Shop depending on the options you choose.
3. **Shipping and storefront:** Complete **shipping** and **storefront** settings in TikTok where prompted so buyers can check out smoothly.
4. **Product status:** In Seller Center, open **product** or **listing** areas and check whether items are **approved**, **pending**, or **need changes**. Fix issues in Shopify; synced updates should flow through after TikTok re-reviews.

More context: [TikTok for Business — TikTok Shop for Shopify](https://ads.tiktok.com/business/en-US/guides/tiktok-shop-shopify-setup-guide).

## If something fails

### “Location address” or catalog sync errors

If Shopify or TikTok says products could not sync because of a **location address** problem:

1. Go to **Settings → [Locations](https://admin.shopify.com/settings/locations)** in Shopify.
2. **Location name:** Keep the name **35 characters or fewer**.
3. **Address:** Make the address match what you see on **Google Maps** for that warehouse or office (same street, city, postal code).
4. **Phone:** Use a valid phone number on the location if required.

If it still fails after correcting this, use **Get Support** inside the TikTok app in Shopify, or contact [TikTok support](https://ads.tiktok.com/athena/requester/boards?identify_key=6a1e079024806640c5e1e695d13db80949525168a052299b4970f9c99cb5ac78).

Details: [Shopify Help — Setting up TikTok Shop (Location address error)](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/online-sales-channels/social-commerce/tiktok/setup#location-address-error).
